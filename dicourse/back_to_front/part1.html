An Introduction (1/7)

The aim of this tutorial is to provide a thorough understanding of creating a website from back-end to front-end. This tutorial has taken many hours to create and perfect-ish. An attempt is made to ensure there are no missing steps between concepts – and as such you should be able to follow along and get to the same results.
